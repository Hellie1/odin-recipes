Risotto/h1>

This is a recipe for a delicious Risotto. It's easy to make and healthy!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Onions
  • Red pepper
  • Mushrooms
  • White rice
  • Chicken stock
  1. Heat a pan and add olive oil.
  2. Chop the onions and add to the pan
  3. Chop the chicken and add to the pan
  4. Chop the peppers and mushrooms and add to the pan
  5. Cook until tender
  6. Add the white rice and dry cook for 1 minute
  7. Slowly add the stock and simmer adding as you go
  8. Cook until the rice is fluffy and the stock has absorbed
