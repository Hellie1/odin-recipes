Beef Stir Fry

This is a recipe for a delicious beef stir fry. It's easy to make and healthy!

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Broccoli
  • Soy Sauce
  • Olive oil
  • Onions
  • Chilli flakes
  1. Heat a shallow frying pan and add olive oil.
  2. Chop the onions and add to the pan
  3. Chop the beef and add to the pan
  4. Chop the brocolli and add to the pan
  5. Cook until cooked
  6. Add chilli flakes to taste
